Implement the information you learned to make your lifestyle more green!

Simple Changes You Can Make at Home:

  • Turn off the light switch when you leave the room!
  • Cut down on eating meat (especially red meat) to learn more about sustainable eating, click here!
  • Don't waste food! Only purchase what you need and eat your leftovers
  • Compost your waste! Some things you can compost include:
  • Vegetable scraps
  • Coffee grounds
  • Cardboard (including cardbord egg cartons)
  • Yard waste
  • To learn more about composting, click here!
  • Recycle! Generally these materials can be recycled:
  • Clean plastic bottles and containers
  • Clean cardboard and paper
  • Tin, steel, and aluminium (includes cans)
  • Clean, unbroken glass
  • To learn more about recycling specifically in your area, click here!
  • Use a reusable water bottle! (This saves money in the long run)
  • Educate yourself on politicians' views and vote with the environment in mind!
  • Try to fix things before throwing them away!
  • If your destination is close enough to walk or bike to, walk or drive to it! Limit your driving!
  • To learn more about the effects your car has on the environment, click here!
  • Use your microwave over your convectional oven
  • Grow your own veggies!
  • Don't throw away your old clothes! You can donate them or put them in the textile recycling bin!
  • Try your best to not fly!

    • Simple Changes You Can Make at Work and School:

  • Use a reusable lunchbox! You can check some out here!
  • Use tupperware and reusable utensils! You can check some out here!
  • Work/take notes online! Go paperless!
  • Walk, carpool, or bike to work/school!
  • Get office plants!
  • Use reusable napkins!
  • Make sustainability a part of your company's/school's culture!

    • Simple Changes You Can Make When Shopping:

  • Shop locally! (Farmer's markets are great for this!)
  • Buy food in season! To check what is in season, click here!
  • Buy used products! Ebay , Craigslist , and thrift stores such as ThredUp are great!
  • Buy products that have recycled packaging!
  • Use reusable bags! (This saves money in the long run)
  • Buy produce without plastic packaging and wash it at home!
  • Only purchase items when you need to!
  • Use cloth diapers instead of plastic diapers!
  • Buy from sustainable brands! You can find some sustainable clothing brands here!
